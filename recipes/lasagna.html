Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna recipe is perfect for any occasion. With layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella, it's a dish that is sure to impress family and friends. Follow this step-by-step guide to create a mouthwatering lasagna that will quickly become a favorite in your household.

Perfect for dinner parties, gatherings, or a hearty weeknight meal, this recipe combines classic Italian flavors with a homemade touch. Serve it with a side of garlic bread and a fresh salad for a complete and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 jar marinara sauce (24 oz)
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add ground beef and Italian sausage, cooking until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce, season with salt and pepper, and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese. Mix until smooth.
  6. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil, if desired.

Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This classic pepperoni pizza recipe is the perfect way to bring the flavors of your favorite pizzeria to your kitchen. With a crispy crust, zesty tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and perfectly spiced pepperoni, this recipe is a crowd-pleaser.

Whether you're making it for a family dinner, a casual get-together, or a Friday night treat, this homemade pizza will be a hit with everyone. Pair it with a fresh side salad or some garlic knots for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Flour for rolling out the dough

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat.
  2. On a floured surface, roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness. Transfer it to a piece of parchment paper for easy handling.
  3. Brush the dough lightly with olive oil, then spread the pizza sauce evenly over the surface, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, ensuring even coverage.
  5. Arrange the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese. Add oregano and red pepper flakes for extra flavor, if desired.
  6. Carefully transfer the pizza (with parchment paper) onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a couple of minutes before slicing and serving.
